Shirts

Who doesn't love a nice shirt? We only print on the top of the line material so you don't have to worry about ordering one of those boxy shirts that feels like cardboard. Afterall, don't you want people to actually wear your brand?

Satellite Printlab uses blanks from Next Level and Bella Canvas. All the research we have done has proven over and over this is where you get the best bang for your buck.

Need something outside of just a tee or a tank? Don't worry we carry American Apparel, Tultex, Nike, OGIO, and more. Just remember, Satellite PrintLab only uses high end blanks with the best printing in Austin.

Embroidered Hats

Embroidery is the most popular for customers in general. However is the most expensive. Always a winner with flat, or puff (3D) style.

This is the strongest and longest lasting method of all the hat styles.

With this hat, art work will need to be done for digitizing. Turn around time is 10 days roughly.

Heat Transfer Hats

Promo Hats are essential to most active brands. Theses shouldn't cost you an arm and a leg since you are probably giving them away!

We can heat transfer an most any hat, if you are looking for price effective and great looking we recommend a foam front trucker hat. These always seem to be winners with the crowds and summer events.
